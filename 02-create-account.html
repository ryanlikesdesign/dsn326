DSN 326 — Step 02

Create a GitHub Account

Sign up is free and takes about 2 minutes.

← Back to Getting Started

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Go to github.com and click the "Sign up" button in the top-right corner.
  2. Enter your email address. Use your school email or a personal email you check regularly.
  3. Create a password. GitHub requires at least 8 characters. Use something you'll remember.
  4. Choose a username. This is important — your username becomes part of your website URL. For example, if your username is janedoe, your site will be at janedoe.github.io.
    Tip: Pick something professional or at least neutral. Your GitHub profile is visible to employers. Avoid joke names. Good examples: jdoe-design, janedoedesign, jane-doe.
  5. Complete the verification puzzle (it will ask you to solve a simple visual puzzle to prove you're human).
  6. Check your email for a verification code from GitHub. Enter the code on the page to confirm your account.
  7. GitHub may ask you a few personalization questions (like "How many team members will work with you?"). You can answer or skip these — they don't affect your account.
  8. Choose the Free plan. When asked to pick a plan, select Free. The free plan includes everything you need for GitHub Pages.
That's it! You now have a GitHub account. Keep your username and password somewhere safe — you'll need them every time you want to update your website.

Quick Checklist

  • I have a GitHub account
  • I remember my username
  • I verified my email address
  • I'm on the Free plan
